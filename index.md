## 📡 Traffic shaping concepts

Traffic shaping refers to the method of pacing network data. By setting limits, you prioritize important traffic. For instance, if you prioritize your web browser, your video calls become smoother because background update processes cannot use all your bandwidth at once. This results in a stable connection for work and entertainment.
